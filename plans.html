Future Plans and Goals

As a start-up, it's very important to have concrete plans and task deadlines to show team consistency for investors. Our team has came up with this long-term plan for start-up launch.

November 4, 2016

Business Idea Camp

This is the place where everything started. This was our first business idea pitch in front of judges, investors and businessmens. Our project won the grand prize, taking 600000KRW into start-up initial fundings.

November - December

Help and Mentorship from IBITP

Currently, our project is on the second stage of evaluation for IBITP mentorship and sponsorship. Our team will be meeting with businessmans twice a week to get feedback and help in start-up issues. If the project will be choosen, we might get up to $5000 to initial startup funds.

Spring 2019

Seed funding

At this stage, our team will pitch "inHanguk" more broadly, attracting as many investors as possible. These fundings are need to establish a company, finish legal issues and prepare for making minimal viable product.

Summer 2019

Startup Launch

Having collected enough fundings for minimal viable product launch, beta-tests and other technical issues will be solved at this phase. Beta-testing would help us to navigate the company in the right direction, following what is important for our users. Initial Google Ads will be testd for integrity and better user experience.
